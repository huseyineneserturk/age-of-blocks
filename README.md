# ⚔️ Age of Blocks

**Age of Blocks** is a free, browser-based, block-focused strategy and conquest game. Build your kingdom, manage your resources, and expand your borders by capturing regions across the map—no downloads required.

👉 **[Play Now in Your Browser! (ageofblocks.games)](https://ageofblocks.games)**

---

## 🎮 About the Game

Age of Blocks combines classic strategy mechanics with a minimalist and modern block design. Instead of dealing with complex menus or waiting for long downloads, you can jump straight into tactical action.

### ✨ Key Features

* **Instant Play:** No installation or high system specifications required. Just open your browser and start playing.
* **Tactical Map Management:** Place blocks strategically, defend your borders, and push deep into enemy lines.
* **Resource & Economy Management:** Every block you conquer grants power and resources. Making the right move at the right time is the key to victory.
* **Easy to Learn, Hard to Master:** The rules are simple, but outsmarting your opponents requires deep strategic planning.

---

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Go to **[ageofblocks.games](https://ageofblocks.games)**.
2. Select your starting region and place your first blocks to kickstart your economy.
3. Target neutral or enemy-controlled blocks to expand your armies and borders.
4. Dominate the entire map to claim total victory!
